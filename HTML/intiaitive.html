OUR INITIATIVE

At E-Cell DMCE, our initiatives are designed to ignite ideas, nurture talent, and empower changemakers. From flagship events that celebrate entrepreneurship, to incubation support and alumni mentorship, each initiative plays a role in shaping a vibrant entrepreneurial ecosystem on campus and beyond.

The Incubation Centre is the helping hand for budding businesses and startups, offering mentorship, resources, funding, and networking to transform ideas into impactful and sustainable ventures.
