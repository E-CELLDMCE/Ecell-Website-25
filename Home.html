WHAT IS E-CELL ?

Entrepreneurship Cell of Datta Meghe College of Engineering is a student-driven community dedicated to promoting innovation, creativity, and the spirit of entrepreneurship. It serves as a platform where students can learn, network, and turn ideas into successful ventures through workshops, competitions, mentorship, and industry collaborations.
